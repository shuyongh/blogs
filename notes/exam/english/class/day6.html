Day6 - 长难句分析实战

约 1748 字大约 6 分钟

2026-05-11

一、长难句的五个"长"的原因

原因说明示例
并列结构多个并列句用 and/but/or 连接A is..., B is..., and C is...
从句嵌套从句套从句主句包含定语从句，定语从句内含状语从句
非谓语动词-ing/-ed/to do 结构的堆叠Faced with..., the man standing there...
插入语用逗号/破折号/括号隔开的成分The fact, however, is...
倒装/省略语序变化Not until...did he...

二、长难句分析三步法

核心方法论

找谓语 → 找连词 → 找核心主句

第一步：找谓语动词

一个谓语 = 一个句子。有几个谓语就有几个"分句"。

谓语可以是：时态动词（is/was/has done/will do）+ 情态动词 + 助动词

第二步：找连词（连接词）

连词类型例子作用
并列连词and, but, or, yet, so连接并列句
从属连词because, although, when, if连接状语从句
关系代词who, which, that, whose连接定语从句
关系副词when, where, why连接定语从句
连接词that, whether, what连接名词性从句

第三步：找出核心主句

去掉各种修饰成分（从句、非谓语、插入语），剩下的就是句子的骨架。

三、实战拆解

例 1：并列结构

The Internet has transformed the way we communicate, and it has also revolutionized the way we do business.

步骤结果
① 找谓语has transformed, has revolutionized
② 找连词and（连接两个并列主句）
③ 核心主句The Internet has transformed...and...has revolutionized...

翻译：互联网改变了我们的沟通方式，也彻底改变了我们的商业模式。

例 2：定语从句嵌套

The findings, which were based on a study that involved more than 10,000 participants, suggest that regular exercise can significantly reduce the risk of heart disease.

步骤结果
① 找谓语were based, involved, suggest, can reduce
② 找连词which（非限制性定语从句），that（定语从句），that（宾语从句）
③ 核心主句The findings suggest that...

结构树：

主句：The findings suggest that...
  ├── which 从句（定语，修饰 findings）：
  │   were based on a study
  │   └── that 从句（定语，修饰 study）：
  │       involved more than 10,000 participants
  └── that 从句（宾语，作 suggest 的宾语）：
      regular exercise can significantly reduce the risk of heart disease

翻译：这些基于一项涉及超过一万名参与者的研究的发现表明，定期运动可以显著降低患心脏病的风险。

例 3：非谓语动词堆叠

Faced with increasingly fierce competition, companies seeking to survive in the global market must constantly innovate, adopting new technologies and developing new products.

步骤结果
① 找谓语must innovate（这是唯一谓语）
② 非谓语Faced with（过去分词），seeking（现在分词），adopting（现在分词），developing（现在分词）
③ 核心主句companies must constantly innovate

结构树：

Faced with increasingly fierce competition（过去分词短语作状语，表原因）
companies seeking to survive in the global market（主语 + 现在分词短语作定语）
must constantly innovate（谓语）
adopting new technologies and developing new products（现在分词短语作伴随状语）

翻译：面对日益激烈的竞争，寻求在全球市场中生存的公司必须不断创新，采用新技术并开发新产品。

例 4：多重从句嵌套

While it is widely acknowledged that globalization has brought unprecedented economic opportunities to developing countries, critics argue that the benefits are not evenly distributed, a view that has gained considerable traction in recent years.

步骤结果
① 找谓语is acknowledged, has brought, argue, are distributed, has gained
② 找连词While（让步状语从句），that（主语从句），that（宾语从句），that（定语从句）
③ 核心主句critics argue that...

结构树：

While（让步状语从句）
  └── it is widely acknowledged（主句）
      └── that globalization has brought...（主语从句，it 是形式主语）
主句：critics argue
  └── that the benefits are not evenly distributed（宾语从句）
      └── a view（同位语，补充说明前面整个观点）
          └── that has gained considerable traction（定语从句，修饰 view）

翻译：尽管人们普遍认为全球化为发展中国家带来了前所未有的经济机遇，但批评者认为利益分配并不均匀——这一观点近年来获得了相当多的支持。

例 5：插入语与分隔结构

The assumption, however, that scientific knowledge is always objective—an idea deeply rooted in the Western tradition—has been increasingly challenged by scholars who emphasize the social nature of scientific practice.

步骤结果
① 找谓语is, has been challenged, emphasize
② 找连词that（同位语从句），who（定语从句）
③ 核心主句The assumption has been increasingly challenged by scholars

注意插入语的剥离：

  • , however, → 副词插入语，去掉
  • that scientific knowledge is always objective → 同位语从句，修饰 assumption
  • —an idea deeply rooted in the Western tradition— → 破折号插入语，补充说明
  • who emphasize the social nature of scientific practice → 定语从句，修饰 scholars

翻译：然而，科学知识总是客观的这一假设——一个深深植根于西方传统的观念——已经越来越多地受到强调科学实践社会性的学者们的质疑。

四、倒装与强调句型

1. 否定词置于句首的倒装

Not until the late 20th century did the significance of environmental protection become widely recognized.

正常语序：The significance of environmental protection did not become widely recognized until the late 20th century.

Hardly had the meeting begun when the power went out. （会议刚开始就停电了。）

2. Only 置于句首的倒装

Only by working together can we overcome these challenges.

3. 强调句型

It is the government that should take responsibility. （是政府应该承担责任。——强调政府）

It was not until the 1960s that the theory gained widespread acceptance. （直到 1960 年代这个理论才获得广泛接受。）

五、综合长难句拆解训练

请用三步法分析下面的句子：

1. The theory that human activities are primarily responsible for climate change, which has been confirmed by numerous scientific studies conducted over the past three decades, forms the basis of international environmental policies.

拆解

谓语：are, has been confirmed, forms 连词：that（同位语从句），which（非限制性定语从句） 核心主句：The theory forms the basis of international environmental policies 翻译：人类活动是气候变化主要原因的理论——这一理论已被过去三十年间进行的无数科学研究证实——构成了国际环境政策的基础。

2. While traditional approaches to education have emphasized the transmission of knowledge from teacher to student, a growing number of educators advocate for a more interactive model, arguing that students learn more effectively when they actively participate in the learning process rather than passively receiving information.

拆解

谓语：have emphasized, advocate, learn, participate 连词：While（让步状语从句），that（宾语从句），when（时间状语从句），rather than（比较） 核心主句：a growing number of educators advocate for a more interactive model 翻译：虽然传统的教育方法强调从教师到学生的知识传递，但越来越多的教育工作者倡导一种更具互动性的模式，他们认为当学生积极参与学习过程而不是被动接收信息时，学习效率更高。

作业

  1. 用三步法分析以下句子：

The concept that intelligence can be measured solely by standardized tests, an assumption that has been widely challenged by contemporary psychologists, fails to capture the multidimensional nature of human cognitive abilities.

  1. 从考研真题阅读中找一句长难句（30词以上），用三步法分析并画出结构树
  2. 练习用中文翻译上述句子，注意语序调整
