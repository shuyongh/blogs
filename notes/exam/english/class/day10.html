Day10 - 真题实战与综合复习

约 1753 字大约 6 分钟

2026-05-11

一、综合阅读实战

Passage:

The relationship between economic growth and environmental sustainability has long been a subject of intense debate. [1] Critics of rapid industrialization argue that the relentless pursuit of GDP growth has come at the expense of ecological degradation, citing alarming rates of deforestation, air pollution, and biodiversity loss as evidence. [2] They contend that the current model of development is fundamentally unsustainable and that governments must prioritize environmental protection over economic expansion.

[3] However, proponents of "green growth" offer a more optimistic perspective. They assert that technological innovation can decouple economic growth from environmental harm. [4] For instance, advances in renewable energy, circular economy practices, and sustainable agriculture have demonstrated that it is possible to reduce environmental impact while maintaining economic prosperity.

[5] A study published in Nature Sustainability examined data from 42 countries over a 20-year period. The findings revealed that countries investing heavily in green technologies experienced an average GDP growth of 3.2% annually—comparable to their conventional counterparts—while reducing carbon emissions by 15%. [6] These results, the researchers suggest, provide compelling evidence that environmental sustainability and economic growth are not mutually exclusive.

[7] Nevertheless, the study also cautioned that green growth is not a panacea. [8] Without strong regulatory frameworks and international cooperation, the benefits of green technology may be unevenly distributed, potentially exacerbating existing inequalities between developed and developing nations.

任务 1：找信号词（3分钟）

在原文中找出以下各类信号词，写在下方：

信号词类型句子编号具体词
转折__________
举例__________
因果__________
对比__________
递进/补充__________
答案
  • 转折：句子 3 → However；句子 7 → Nevertheless
  • 举例：句子 4 → For instance
  • 对比：句子 5 → comparable to...while
  • 递进/补充：句子 6 → also
  • 因果：句子 6 → provide evidence that（虽然不直接，但属于说明关系）

其他：句子 5 的 revealed that（研究结果），句子 1 的 argue（观点表达）

任务 2：长难句分析（5分钟）

分析句子 5：

A study published in Nature Sustainability examined data from 42 countries over a 20-year period.

三步法分析：

步骤结果
① 找谓语examined
② 找连词无（简单句）
③ 核心主句A study examined data

修饰成分分析：

  • published in Nature Sustainability → 过去分词短语作定语，修饰 study
  • from 42 countries → 介词短语作定语，修饰 data
  • over a 20-year period → 介词短语作时间状语

翻译：一项发表在《自然·可持续发展》上的研究调查了 42 个国家在 20 年间的数据。

任务 3：词汇分级（3分钟）

将以下从文章中选出的词汇分级：

relentless, degradation, deforestation, biodiversity, unsustainable, proponents, perspective, innovation, decouple, circular, compelling, mutually exclusive, panacea, regulatory, exacerbate, inequalities

写作词汇（⭐）：

词原因
perspective写作中表达"观点"
compelling形容论证有说服力
mutualmutual respect/understanding
exclusively常用于否定句

阅读词汇（📖）：

词意思
relentless不懈的，持续的
degradation退化，恶化
deforestation森林砍伐
biodiversity生物多样性
unsustainable不可持续的
proponents支持者
innovation创新
decouple分离，脱钩
exacerbate加剧，恶化
inequalities不平等

推断词汇（🔍）：

词推断方法
panacea上下文说"not a panacea"，且前面说"green growth"，推测 ="万能药"
circular economycircular（循环的）+ economy → 循环经济

任务 4：阅读理解题

Q1: What is the main idea of the passage? [A] Economic growth inevitably harms the environment. [B] Green growth can solve all environmental problems. [C] Environmental sustainability and economic growth can coexist with proper measures. ✅ [D] Developing countries should prioritize economic growth over environment.

解析

答案：C

文章结构：转折信号词 However（句子3）引出全文核心观点——绿色增长可以实现经济增长与环境保护的双赢。句子 6 和句子 8 进一步明确：两者并非互斥，但需要监管和国际合作。

Q2: According to the study published in Nature Sustainability, countries investing in green technologies: [A] Experienced slower economic growth. [B] Reduced carbon emissions by 15% while maintaining comparable GDP growth. ✅ [C] Failed to achieve environmental benefits. [D] Only benefited developed nations.

解析

答案：B

定位词 Nature Sustainability 定位到句子 5-6。研究结果显示：年 GDP 增长 3.2%（与常规国家相当）同时减排 15%。

Q3: What does the word "panacea" (line 1, sentence 7) most probably mean? [A] A solution to all problems. ✅ [B] A type of environmental policy. [C] A form of renewable energy. [D] A regulatory framework.

解析

答案：A

猜词线索：上文讲绿色增长的好处，但句子 7 开头 Nevertheless（转折）提醒这里要指出不足。not a panacea + 下文"需要监管框架和国际合作" → 暗示绿色增长不能解决一切问题，所以 panacea = 万能药。

二、完形填空实战

Directions: Choose the best word for each blank.

Many students find English vocabulary learning to be a daunting task. 1, with the right strategies, it can become an enjoyable and rewarding experience. One effective approach is to learn words 2 context rather than memorizing isolated word lists.

Research has shown that learners who encounter new words in authentic reading materials retain them more 3 than those who use rote memorization. This is because contextual learning helps establish meaningful 4 between new words and existing knowledge.

5, it is important to review words at regular intervals. The "spacing effect"—a phenomenon well-documented in cognitive psychology—suggests that information is better retained when study sessions are 6 out over time.

1. [A] Therefore　[B] However ✅　[C] Additionally　[D] Consequently

2. [A] on　[B] at　[C] in ✅　[D] by

3. [A) effectively ✅　[B] effortless　[C] efficient　[D] essential

4. [A] connections ✅　[B] conflicts　[C] competitions　[D] conclusions

5. [A] For example　[B] In addition ✅　[C] On the contrary　[D] As a result

6. [A] carried　[B] taken　[C] spaced ✅　[D] put

答案解析

1. B — 前句"困难" vs 后句"可以很愉快" → 转折。

2. C — in context（在语境中），固定搭配。

3. A — 修饰 retain（记忆），需要副词。effectively（有效地）修饰动词。

4. A — establish connections between（建立联系），动宾搭配。

5. B — 前文讲"语境学习"，后文讲"定期复习" → 递进关系。

6. C — spaced out（分散开），固定搭配。spacing effect（间隔效应）是心理学专有术语。
